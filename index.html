missed connections - (long-lost) Friend

I tried writing you a letter a few years ago, in a fit of desperation. I regret that I didn't write one sooner. I drafted another letter, and then wrote more as time passed, but didn't send them for a while. Finally walking to the post office and folding an envelope into the mail slot almost felt religious.

It's been months, but I haven't gotten a response since. Maybe the address was wrong, maybe it was lost in the mail, maybe you just aren't there. It's impossible to know for sure, but I still feel like I messed something up? I don't really understand.

Anyways, if you have time, could you let me know? I know I talk about myself a lot but I really, truly want to know what you're thinking, what you've been up to these past years. What's on your mind? Maybe we could get together, play some games, go on a walk, or just stay home. I can let you pick this time.
